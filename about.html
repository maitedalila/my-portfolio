Bio

I am a Product Desginer based in Miami, FL. I've been a digital nomad for the past 7 years living in 6 cities, 3 countries and 3 continents. While traveling, I have learned the importance of having different perspectives and some of the most rewarding work has happened because I was challenged by an idea.

At work, I develop UI requirements from concept to release. I define features, user flows, and content. My goal is to create enjoyable, seamless experiences.
Besides being interested in internet things, I have got a particular fondness for traveling, wildlife and sustainability.

I believe that in every line of code written, every interaction made, every word read, every visual seen, the user should always be taken into consideration.
