Process

Understand Users

identify the users, their objectives and behaviors.

Sythesize Data

identify insights in the form themes, patterns, and opportunities.

Design Sprints

structured brainstorm based on design thinking and agile development

Design and Build

create visual styles and structure.

Prototype and Refine

tests a concept or process, help communicates it to stakeholders and illustrates motion to developers.

Iterate

measure data against hypothesis, understand WHY it happened, form a new hypothesis and tweak the design
